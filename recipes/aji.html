El Ají de La Abuela

Description

Ají is Colombian salsa that can be made in a variety of ways with various ingredients. However, this is the recipe that my abuela stood by. It goes great with many Colombian dishes, including Los Chorizos de Bernardo. It is one of my favorite salsas ever.

Ingredients

  • 2 red peppers (remove seeds)
  • 2-3 jalapeños (remove seeds)
  • cilantro
  • 2 lemons or limes
  • 1/2 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • 1 small white onion
  • salt to taste
  • pepper optional
  • 1/2 cup of cilantro

Steps

  1. Blend (roughly) red peppers, jalapeños, cilantro, lemon juice, and red wine vinegar.
  2. Add green onions, white onions, all finely chopped, salt to taste, and lots of cilantro.
